Hi, I'm Patrick Cartelli

Product Designer · MVP Design for Technical Founders · 15+ Years B2B SaaS

I started Studio Cartelli because I kept seeing the same pattern: talented founders with validated ideas and real market opportunity, stuck between overpriced agencies and inexperienced freelancers. They didn't need a rebrand or a full-service team. They needed focused, strategic design expertise to get their MVP shipped.

I've spent 15+ years designing B2B SaaS products at companies like Vividly and Dataminr. My approach is simple: research before I touch Figma, solutions that don't make users think, and developer-ready files your engineers can actually use.

Outside of design, I've spent years embedded in music and photography: making things, building communities, staying close to what moves people. There's something about discovering an artist through a community instead of an algorithm that shapes how I think about building products. The best ones bring people together.
